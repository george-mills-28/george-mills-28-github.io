Emotional Intelligence


What is Emotional Intelligence?

  • Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is the ability to recognize, understand, and manage our own emotions and the emotions of others. It involves mindfulness, self-knowledge, and creating positive mental habits.

    • How is EQ Different from IQ ?

  • IQ measures intellectual abilities, such as logic and reasoning, while EQ focuses on emotional awareness and interpersonal skills. Unlike IQ, which is more fixed, EQ can be developed through practice.

    • Why is EQ Important ?

  • EQ is crucial for personal and professional success. It enhances performance, leadership, and overall well-being, making it essential in complex, human-centered environments.
